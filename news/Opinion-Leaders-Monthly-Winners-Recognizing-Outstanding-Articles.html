Starting immediately, from 01 December 2023, we are replacing the previous daily reward of 10 FCOIN per person with a dynamic rewards program that centers around the quality of your contributions.

We believe that high-quality content deserves greater recognition, and so we have established a new tiered rewards structure ranging from 50 to 200 FCOIN for outstanding articles.

What makes an article eligible for these enhanced rewards? Here are the criteria:

1.Originality and Exclusivity: Your content must be fresh and unpublished elsewhere.

 
2.Visual Appeal and Presentation: Articles should have clear and orderly text with engaging visuals. They must be well-formatted, with a minimum length of 200 words. Videos should be clear, smooth, and have a duration of at least 3 minutes.
 
3.Coherence and Focus: Your article should have a clear and organized structure, with distinct paragraphs that explore a specific trading theme. Avoid tangents and ensure your content stays focused.
 
4.Insightful and Inspiring: We value content that offers new perspectives, trading strategies, and methods that spark inspiration and provide valuable insights to our community.

 

The rewards are directly proportional to the quality of your content. The higher the quality, the greater the rewards you can earn!
